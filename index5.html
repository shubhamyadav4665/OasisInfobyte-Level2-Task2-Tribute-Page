Biography

Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the cofounder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc; CEO and largest shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios; and founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT Inc. Jobs is widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s, along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak. His creative vision and relentless passion reshaped six major industries: personal computers, animated movies, music, phones, tablet computing, and digital publishing.
